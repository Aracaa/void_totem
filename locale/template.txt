# textdomain: void_totem

Totem of Void Undying=
Protects you from void death while wielding it=
A totem of void undying is a rare artifact which may safe you from void death.=
The totem only works while you hold it in your hand. If you die in the void, you will be teleported back to the surfact with 1 HP. The totem is destroyed in the process, however.=
